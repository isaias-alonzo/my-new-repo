December 18th in History.

1787: New Jersey became the third state admitted to the United States when it ratified the U.S. Constitution.

1892: Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky's ballet The Nutcracker was first presented at the Mariinsky Theatre in St. Petersburg.

1966: The TV special How the Grinch Stole Christmas!, an adaptation of Dr. Seuss's classic children's book, aired for the first time and became a holiday staple.
